Use original content - personal stories are powerful and extremely relatable

Use a visual - you never know whose life you may touch

Add the hashtag: #LifeInTheZone to your story

You can either upload your story direct to the website or embed the link of an existing story from your social media channels (Twitter, YouTube, Instagram). Simply copy the link from the preferred channel and paste it into the #LifeInTheZone story submission section to create a story

Please Note: You can only embed stories from public social media profiles. Please follow the instructions below to learn how.

Instagram:

Find the Instagram post that you would like to share. Click on the three dots on the upper right corner of the post, then click ‘Go to post’. Copy the post’s link from the browser address bar and paste it here. Be sure that the post is publicly available.

For more information, click here: https://help.instagram.com/372819389498306

YouTube:

Find the YouTube video that you would like to share, then copy the video’s link from the browser address bar and paste it here. Be sure that the video is not part of a playlist and is publicly available. The link should be a standard YouTube link, meaning youtu.be or other URL shorteners shouldn’t be used.

For more information, click here: https://support.google.com/youtube/answer/171780

Twitter:

Find the Tweet that you would like to share. Copy the Tweet’s link from the browser address bar and paste it here. Be sure that you copy the link to the Tweet, not just the photo, and that the Tweet is publicly available.

For more information, click here: https://help.twitter.com/en/using-twitter/tweet-and-moment-url
